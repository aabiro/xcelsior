## What is Xcelsior?

Xcelsior is a distributed GPU orchestration platform that aggregates consumer and data-center GPUs into a managed compute marketplace. Built for **Canadian data sovereignty**, it provides PIPEDA and Quebec Law 25 compliance out of the box.

## How It Works

1. **Providers** register GPU hosts via the worker agent. Hosts undergo automated admission gating (driver versions, security checks).
2. **Submitters** submit AI/ML jobs specifying VRAM requirements, priority, and isolation tier.
3. **The scheduler** matches jobs to admitted hosts based on capacity, trust tier, and data residency rules.
4. **Billing** handles CAD-native pricing with 13-province GST/HST, wallet credits, and Stripe Connect payouts.
