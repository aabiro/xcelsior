## HPC Meets Sovereign Cloud

High-performance computing workflows — molecular dynamics, climate modelling, large-scale training — have traditionally required dedicated cluster access. That often means US-based cloud HPC services or months-long procurement for on-prem hardware.

Today we're launching **HPC / Slurm integration** on Xcelsior, bringing familiar cluster computing workflows to our sovereign GPU marketplace.

## What's New

- **Cluster Profiles** — Choose from pre-configured cluster profiles optimised for different workload types (ML training, inference, simulation, general HPC).
- **Slurm-Compatible Job Submission** — Submit jobs using familiar Slurm parameters: nodes, GPUs per node, walltime, partitions.
- **Real-Time Status** — Track job status from pending through running to completion, with live telemetry.
- **Cancel & Resubmit** — Full job lifecycle management from the dashboard.

## How It Works

1. Navigate to **Dashboard → HPC / Slurm**
2. Select a cluster profile that matches your workload
3. Fill in job parameters (name, nodes, GPUs, walltime)
4. Paste your job script
5. Submit — Xcelsior's scheduler matches your job to available hardware

Behind the scenes, our scheduler communicates with a Slurm adapter that translates between Xcelsior's marketplace model and standard Slurm job management. This means you get the flexibility of a shared marketplace with the familiarity of Slurm.

## Sovereignty by Default

Every HPC job on Xcelsior runs on Canadian hardware, under Canadian law, with province-level routing when required. Your simulation data, model checkpoints, and job artifacts never leave Canadian jurisdiction.

## Get Started

HPC / Slurm is available now for all Xcelsior accounts. Visit your [dashboard](/dashboard/hpc) to submit your first job.
