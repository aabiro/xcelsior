## What Is the AI Compute Access Fund?

The Government of Canada's **$300M AI Compute Access Fund** subsidises access to compute infrastructure for Canadian researchers, startups, and public-sector organisations. Eligible applicants can receive rebates of up to **67% of compute costs** — but only when using qualifying Canadian infrastructure.

## Qualifying for the Maximum Rebate

The Fund offers two rebate tiers:

| Infrastructure Type | Rebate |
|---|---|
| Canadian-owned infrastructure | **67%** |
| Non-Canadian (e.g., AWS Canada) | 50% |

To qualify for the higher tier, your compute provider must be:
- Incorporated in Canada
- Operating infrastructure physically located in Canada
- Not subject to foreign data disclosure laws

Xcelsior meets all three criteria.

## How Xcelsior Makes It Easy

We've aligned our invoicing directly with the Fund's eligible cost categories:

1. **GPU-hour charges** map to "Compute Access" category
2. **Storage and artifact fees** map to "Data Management" category
3. **Support and SLA premiums** map to "Infrastructure Services" category

Every invoice includes a downloadable **Supplier Attestation Pack** — a pre-filled document set that your grants office can submit directly with your Fund application.

## Step-by-Step

1. Sign up at [xcelsior.ca/register](/register)
2. Submit compute jobs as normal
3. Go to **Billing → Invoices** and click "Download Fund Pack" on any invoice
4. Submit the pack with your AI Compute Access Fund application
5. Receive up to 67% back
