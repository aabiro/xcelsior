## Why the Gap Exists

Two structural reasons explain the price difference:

### 1. No Hyperscaler Margin

AWS, Google, and Azure price GPU compute with 60-80% gross margins. Xcelsior operates as a marketplace connecting Canadian GPU owners directly with users. The platform takes a transparent commission, and competitive supply keeps prices down.

### 2. Canadian Dollar Advantage

AWS prices in USD. When the Canadian dollar sits at 0.72-0.74 USD (as it has for most of 2025-2026), every AWS bill comes with a built-in 30%+ currency penalty for Canadian businesses. Xcelsior prices natively in CAD.
